Key IRS Relief Programs to Know

  • Offer in Compromise — Settle your full IRS balance for less than you owe. Average settlement: 20 cents on the dollar.
  • Installment Agreement — Pay over up to 72 months. Stops wage garnishment immediately upon approval.
  • Currently Not Collectible — Freeze all IRS collection if you have no disposable income after living expenses.
  • Penalty Abatement — Remove failure-to-file and failure-to-pay penalties. Can reduce your balance by 25-50%.
  • Innocent Spouse Relief — Remove your liability from a jointly filed return when your spouse created the debt.
