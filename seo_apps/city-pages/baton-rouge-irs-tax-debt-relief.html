How IRS Tax Debt Works & Your Options

The IRS Collection Process

If you owe back taxes, the IRS follows a specific collection timeline. Understanding this process helps you take action before your situation gets worse:

  1. Notice & Demand (Notice CP3): You receive a bill for taxes owed
  2. IRS Lien: The IRS files a tax lien against your property (in LA records)
  3. Levy: The IRS levies your bank account or wages to collect the debt
  4. Property Seizure: In extreme cases, the IRS may seize and sell your assets
⚠️ Important: The IRS has 10 years to collect, but they can extend this period. The sooner you act, the better your options. Contact us at (858) 610-9779 for immediate assistance.
