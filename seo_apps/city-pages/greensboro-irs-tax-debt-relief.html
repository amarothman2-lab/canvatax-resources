6 Proven IRS Tax Relief Programs Available in Greensboro

1. Offer in Compromise (OIC) – The Most Powerful Option

Settle your IRS tax debt for significantly less than you owe. If you qualify, we negotiate with the IRS to accept a lump-sum payment, typically reducing your liability by 40-70%. Greensboro residents with financial hardship frequently qualify.

2. Installment Agreements (Payment Plans)

Can't pay in one lump sum? Set up a structured monthly payment plan with the IRS. Options include short-term (180 days) or long-term (up to 6 years) agreements. We ensure your payment is affordable based on your income and expenses.

3. Currently Not Collectible (CNC) Status

If financial hardship prevents payment now, CNC temporarily stops IRS enforcement. Wage garnishments and bank levies pause while interest and penalties continue to accrue. CNC typically lasts 3 years and can be renewed if hardship persists.

4. Wage Garnishment Release

The IRS taking 70% of your paycheck? We file for immediate wage garnishment release, restoring your take-home pay. Most releases occur within 30-45 days of approval.

5. Bank Levy Release

Bank account frozen by the IRS? We petition for immediate levy release, recovering your funds and protecting future deposits. Levies typically release within 21 days.

6. IRS Audit Representation

Facing an audit? Our experienced representatives handle all IRS correspondence, documentation, and negotiation—so you never have to face the IRS alone.
