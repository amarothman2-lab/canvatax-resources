Our 6 Major IRS Tax Debt Relief Programs

1. Offer in Compromise (OIC)

Settle your IRS tax debt for less than you owe. If you qualify, we negotiate with the IRS to accept a lump-sum settlement, often reducing your total liability by 40-70%. Lubbock businesses and individuals with cash-flow challenges frequently benefit from OIC.

2. Installment Agreements (Payment Plans)

Can't pay in full? Set up a structured payment plan directly with the IRS. We handle all negotiations so you pay what's manageable, not what's punitive. Short-term plans (180 days) or long-term agreements (up to 6 years) available.

3. Currently Not Collectible (CNC) Status

If your financial hardship prevents payment right now, CNC temporarily pauses IRS collection efforts. Interest and penalties still accrue, but enforcement stops—giving you time to stabilize. CNC lasts up to 3 years and can be renewed.

4. Wage Garnishment Release

The IRS is taking up to 70% of your paycheck? We petition for wage garnishment release, restoring your take-home pay. Most garnishments are lifted within 30-45 days once approved.

5. Bank Levy Release

The IRS froze your bank account? We file for immediate levy release, recovering your funds and protecting future deposits. Levies typically release within 21 days.

6. IRS Audit Representation

Facing an IRS audit? Our experienced representatives handle all correspondence, documentation, and negotiation—you don't have to face the IRS alone.
