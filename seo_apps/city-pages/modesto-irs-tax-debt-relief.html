Most powerful tool: negotiate with the IRS to accept a lump-sum settlement, often 40-70% less than your total debt. Modesto residents with financial hardship frequently qualify. We handle the entire negotiation.

2. Installment Agreements (Monthly Payment Plans)

Temporarily pause IRS enforcement while you stabilize financially. Interest and penalties continue to accrue, but wage garnishments and bank levies stop. CNC typically lasts 3 years and can be renewed.
