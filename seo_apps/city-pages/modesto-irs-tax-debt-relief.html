6 Proven IRS Tax Relief Programs We Offer

1. Offer in Compromise (OIC) – Settle for Pennies on the Dollar

Most powerful tool: negotiate with the IRS to accept a lump-sum settlement, often 40-70% less than your total debt. Modesto residents with financial hardship frequently qualify. We handle the entire negotiation.

2. Installment Agreements (Monthly Payment Plans)

Prefer monthly payments? Set up a structured plan with flexible terms. Short-term (180 days) or long-term (up to 6 years) agreements available. We ensure your payment is affordable based on your income.

3. Currently Not Collectible (CNC) Status

Temporarily pause IRS enforcement while you stabilize financially. Interest and penalties continue to accrue, but wage garnishments and bank levies stop. CNC typically lasts 3 years and can be renewed.

4. Stop Wage Garnishment

The IRS is taking up to 70% of your paycheck? We file for immediate wage garnishment release. Most garnishments lift within 30-45 days, restoring your full take-home pay.

5. Bank Levy Release

Bank account frozen? We petition for immediate levy release. Your funds are typically returned within 21 days, and we protect future deposits.

6. IRS Audit Representation

Facing an audit? Our experienced representatives handle all IRS communication, documentation gathering, and negotiation—so you don't have to.
