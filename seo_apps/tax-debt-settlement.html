How Tax Debt Settlement Actually Works

Step 1: Understanding Your Full Tax Liability

Before negotiating with the IRS, you need a complete picture of what you owe — including all years, all penalties, all interest. We pull your IRS account transcript to get the full balance and identify any discrepancies or errors in what the IRS claims you owe.

Step 2: Matching You to the Right Program

Not every taxpayer qualifies for every program. An Offer in Compromise requires limited assets and income. An installment agreement is available to almost everyone. A professional review ensures you pursue the program that achieves the best outcome — not just the one you heard about in an ad.

Step 3: Negotiating and Executing the Resolution

Once we identify the right path, we handle all IRS communication, paperwork, and negotiation. For OICs, this means calculating the correct offer amount and presenting your financials accurately. For installment agreements, it means negotiating the lowest monthly payment the IRS will accept based on your actual expenses.

Why CanvaTax Is Different
